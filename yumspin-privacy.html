Yumspin - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026 - 中文版见下方

The short version

Yumspin does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and no server of our own.

Location

If you grant location access, it is used only on your device to search nearby restaurants through Apple Maps (MapKit). Your queries are handled by Apple under Apple's privacy policy. We never receive or store your location.

Your preferences

Your blocklist, custom wheel entries, search radius and language setting are stored locally on your iPhone and are deleted when you delete the app.

Children

Yumspin is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone.

Contact

Questions, feedback or business inquiries: x612uia@gmail.com

Yumspin（今天吃啥）- 隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 7 月 15 日

简要版

「今天吃啥」不收集、不存储、不传输任何个人数据。App 没有账号系统、没有统计分析、没有广告、没有追踪，也没有我们自己的服务器。

位置信息

如果你授予位置权限，它只在你的设备上用于通过 Apple 地图（MapKit）搜索附近店家。查询由 Apple 按照其隐私政策处理，我们不会收到或存储你的位置。

你的偏好

屏蔽名单、自定义转盘选项、搜索范围和语言设置只保存在你的 iPhone 本地，删除 App 即全部删除。

儿童

本 App 适合所有年龄，不收集任何人的数据。
